### 魔方的开通

方式：

使用**“京麦”**登录主账号，点击**“服务市场”**输入**“客服魔方”**进行搜索

![image-20220214153051768](https://github.com/1024580117/JDKFJC/blob/main/typora-user-images/image-20220214153051768.png)

选择与“**店铺相对应性质**”的选项，开通时间，会显示对应的金额，去付款开通即可

![image-20220214153158365](https://github.com/1024580117/JDKFJC/blob/main/typora-user-images/image-20220214153158365.png)

### 咚咚催付

#### **添加客服**

客服魔方内添加客服账号，路径：**系统管理👉系统设置👉客服设置**

![image-20220214153852074](https://github.com/1024580117/JDKFJC/blob/main/typora-user-images/image-20220214153852074.png)

非shop后台创建的客服账号，只能通过手动进行添加

![image-20220214154044272](https://github.com/1024580117/JDKFJC/blob/main/typora-user-images/image-20220214154044272.png)

#### **插件添加**

打开**“客服咚咚”**页面，在右边**“+”**号点开，选择客服魔方插件添加

![image-20220214154933391](https://github.com/1024580117/JDKFJC/blob/main/typora-user-images/image-20220214154933391.png)

#### **绩效明细**

点击**绩效明细**，选择对应**客服昵称**

![image-20220214155146815](https://github.com/1024580117/JDKFJC/blob/main/typora-user-images/image-20220214155146815.png)

选好客服后选择数据**指标类型**

![image-20220214155254917](https://github.com/1024580117/JDKFJC/blob/main/typora-user-images/image-20220214155254917.png)

选择对应顾客进行催付，也可查看响应对话

![image-20220214155507379](https://github.com/1024580117/JDKFJC/blob/main/typora-user-images/image-20220214155507379.png)
